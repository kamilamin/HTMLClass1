FILM MAKING TERMS

Arc Shot
Interlude
Prologue

ARC-SHOT

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera

INTERLUDE

A brief, intervening film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears with in a film

PROLOGUE

A speech, preface, introduction, or brief scene preceding the main action or plot of the film; contrast to epilogue
