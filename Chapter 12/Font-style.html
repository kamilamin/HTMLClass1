Briard

The braird, or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep

Breed History

The braird, which is believed to have originated in france, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was by the French army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing. braird were used in the first world war almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of braird is slowly recovering. Charlemagne, napoleon, Thomas Jefferson and Lafayette all owned brairds.
